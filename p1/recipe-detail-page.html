Blueberry Banana Pancakes

(25) | 104

Indulge in a delicious breakfast treat with our scrumptious Blueberry Banana Pancakes. Our recipe maker will guide you through the simple steps to create a stack of fluffy, tender pancakes bursting with juicy blueberries and sweet banana slices. The perfect combination of tart and sweet, these pancakes are sure to become a family favorite.

prep time

30 min

cook time

15 min

serving size

6

Ingredients

  1. Ripe Banana
    110 g
  2. Breeze Almond Milk
    0.75 cup
  3. Vanilla
    1 tsp
  4. Flour
    90 g
  5. Baking powder
    1 tsp
  1. Cinnamon
    25 g
  2. Salt
    1 g
  3. Blueberries
    25 g
  4. Coconut Oil
    2 g

Instructions

Step 1:
In a small food processor, puree the banana with the almond milk and vanilla. (or mash it really well by hand - you want it pretty liquified).

Step 2:
In a separate medium sized bowl, mix together the dry ingredients. Pour the banana/milk mixture into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Gently stir in the blueberries.

Step 3:
Heat a few teaspoons of coconut oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Using a Â¼ cup measuring cup, scoop the pancake batter into the pan. Flip when bubbles form in the batter (about 1 minute). Let the second side cook for about 60 seconds and remove from heat.

Step 4:
For the blueberry topping, place blueberries in a small bowl with a little bit of maple syrup. Microwave for 20-30 seconds or until they burst a little.

Step 5:
Serve pancakes warm with blueberry sauce and maple syrup.
